# Target Roles

## Primary Targets

- Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- Chief AI Officer
- Chief Digital Officer
- Chief Information Officer (CIO)
- EVP Technology

---

## Secondary Targets

- SVP Technology
- VP Engineering
- VP Networks
- VP Digital Transformation
- Managing Director Technology

---

## Strategic Fit Criteria

Roles must include at least one of:

- Technology strategy ownership
- Large-scale transformation responsibility
- AI / automation / digital evolution mandate
- International scope
- Executive committee exposure
